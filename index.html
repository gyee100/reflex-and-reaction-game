Welcome to reflex & reaction tester!

The game is simple. All you have to do is click on as many correct boxes or circles as possible within your chosen time frame. Each time you get one correct you get a point, and for every time get one wrong you lose a point.

To advance to level two you need to score a minimum score of 25. To beat level two and win the game, you need to score a minimum of 50 and aseceret extra twist. Good luck and have some fun!
